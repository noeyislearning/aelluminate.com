Data Science is revolutionizing the way we understand and interact with the world. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply curious about how data can be used to solve real-world problems, this course is designed to provide a comprehensive introduction to Data Science and its applications. Over the course of four weeks, we will explore the fundamental concepts of Data Science, delve into real-world case studies, and engage in lively discussions to understand how data-driven insights can drive decision-making and innovation.

This course is designed to provide a comprehensive introduction to Data Science, focusing on its applications across various fields. Through engaging case studies and lively discussions, students will gain a deeper understanding of how data-driven insights can drive decision-making and innovation. Whether you're looking to enhance your career, explore new interests, or simply satisfy your curiosity, this course offers a valuable opportunity to learn and discuss the transformative power of Data Science.
